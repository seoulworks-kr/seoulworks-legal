2. Information We Collect

K-Tarot: Seoul Cards is designed to respect your privacy. We do not operate any servers and do not collect personal data on our own systems.

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your tarot reading history (last 20 readings)

This data stays on your device. If you uninstall the App, this data is deleted.

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect or process limited information:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

For free users, we display banner advertisements and optional rewarded video advertisements through Google AdMob. In accordance with Google Mobile Ads SDK disclosures, AdMob may collect or process the following:

  • Device and account identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID (AAID) where available
  • IP address, which may be used to estimate your general (country/region-level) location
  • Approximate device information (device model, OS version, language, screen size)
  • App and ad interaction information, such as app launches, ad impressions, clicks/taps, and rewarded video views

Google AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID.

Founder's Pack users do not see advertisements. Where technically possible, the App avoids requesting or displaying AdMob ads for users who have an active Founder's Pack entitlement.

Advertising Consent (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

Where required by law (including the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland), users may be asked to provide consent before personalized advertisements are shown. Specifically:

  • You may update or withdraw your advertising consent choices through the App's privacy options where available, your device's Google advertising settings, or Google's Ads Settings page at adssettings.google.com.
  • If you decline personalized ads, non-personalized ads may still be shown to support the free version of the App.

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

When you purchase the Founder's Pack ($3.99 one-time purchase), the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles all payment information directly — we never see or store your payment details.

Google Play's billing practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
